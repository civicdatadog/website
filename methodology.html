Plain-Language Summary

Data is sourced from public government records and published as static HTML for auditability.

AI is used to normalize formats, categorize spending, and detect duplicate records.

No estimates, projections, or opinions are added.

Data Infrastructure

Data ingestion and processing is powered by Athena.live.

Civic Watchdog republishes verified public records without interpretation.
