Maryland Government Spending Data Sources

Access official Maryland government spending data through the following authoritative sources. These portals provide state budget information, spending records, transparency data, and public records for Maryland.

All data sources are official government websites. For federal funds flowing into Maryland, see USAspending.gov filtered by state.

Official Maryland Data Sources

  • State Transparency Portal / Open Data - Maryland state transparency portal and open data access
  • State Auditor / Comptroller - Maryland state auditor office with audited spending reports and financial records
  • Budget Office / Management & Budget - Maryland state budget office with budget documents and spending reports
  • USAspending.gov - Federal funds flowing into Maryland (grants, contracts, assistance) - Filter by state: MD

About Maryland Spending Data

Maryland government spending data includes state budget information, agency spending records, contracts, grants, and local government financial data. All data is compiled from official Maryland state sources and federal spending databases.

For comprehensive information about government spending transparency, see the Sources page. For methodology on how data is collected and published, see Methodology.
