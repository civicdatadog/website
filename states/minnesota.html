Minnesota Government Spending Data Sources

Access official Minnesota government spending data through the following authoritative sources. These portals provide state budget information, spending records, transparency data, and public records for Minnesota.

All data sources are official government websites. For federal funds flowing into Minnesota, see USAspending.gov filtered by state.

Official Minnesota Data Sources

  • State Transparency Portal / Open Data - Minnesota state transparency portal and open data access
  • State Auditor / Comptroller - Minnesota state auditor office with audited spending reports and financial records
  • Budget Office / Management & Budget - Minnesota state budget office with budget documents and spending reports
  • USAspending.gov - Federal funds flowing into Minnesota (grants, contracts, assistance) - Filter by state: MN

About Minnesota Spending Data

Minnesota government spending data includes state budget information, agency spending records, contracts, grants, and local government financial data. All data is compiled from official Minnesota state sources and federal spending databases.

For comprehensive information about government spending transparency, see the Sources page. For methodology on how data is collected and published, see Methodology.
