New Mexico Government Spending Data Sources

Access official New Mexico government spending data through the following authoritative sources. These portals provide state budget information, spending records, transparency data, and public records for New Mexico.

All data sources are official government websites. For federal funds flowing into New Mexico, see USAspending.gov filtered by state.

Official New Mexico Data Sources

  • State Transparency Portal / Open Data - New Mexico state transparency portal and open data access
  • State Auditor / Comptroller - New Mexico state auditor office with audited spending reports and financial records
  • Budget Office / Management & Budget - New Mexico state budget office with budget documents and spending reports
  • USAspending.gov - Federal funds flowing into New Mexico (grants, contracts, assistance) - Filter by state: NM

About New Mexico Spending Data

New Mexico government spending data includes state budget information, agency spending records, contracts, grants, and local government financial data. All data is compiled from official New Mexico state sources and federal spending databases.

For comprehensive information about government spending transparency, see the Sources page. For methodology on how data is collected and published, see Methodology.
