Mission

Civic Datadog makes government spending transparent, searchable, and public. Our platform provides comprehensive access to federal spending, state spending, and local spending data compiled from verified public records and government sources across all US governments.

Civic Datadog does not interpret data — it exposes it. We focus on government transparency, budget transparency, and fiscal transparency through neutral, verifiable public data aggregation. All pages are pre-generated static HTML — no JavaScript frameworks, no dashboards, no tracking.

Spending Categories

Explore comprehensive government spending data organized by jurisdiction. All spending records are sourced from official government budgets, financial reports, and public records.

  • Federal spending - Federal government budget data, agency obligations, and program spending
  • State spending - State government budgets, state agency spending, and state-level financial data
  • Local spending - City budgets, county spending, and local government financial records

Browse datasets by jurisdiction and fiscal year. All data is published as static HTML for public auditability and transparency.
