## About

Civic Datadog is a government spending transparency platform that makes public spending data transparent, searchable, and accessible. We provide comprehensive access to federal spending, state spending, and local spending records compiled from verified public sources.

## Mission

Civic Datadog makes government spending transparent, searchable, and public. We focus on government transparency, budget transparency, and fiscal transparency by republishing official government records without interpretation or bias.

## Features

- **Federal Spending Data** - Federal government spending records, agency obligations, and program budgets
- **State Spending Data** - State government spending records and state-level fiscal data
- **Local Spending Data** - City and county spending data and municipal financial records
- **Agency Directory** - Comprehensive directory of government agencies with reported spending amounts
- **Transparent Methodology** - Clear explanation of how data is collected, normalized, and published
- **Verified Sources** - All data sourced from official government APIs, budget reports, and public records

## Data Sources

All spending data is compiled from verified public sources including:

- **Government APIs:** USAspending API, Data.gov API catalog
- **Budget Reports:** Office of Management and Budget, Congressional Budget Office, state budget offices
- **Financial Disclosures:** SEC EDGAR, Government Accountability Office reports
- **Public Datasets:** Data.gov catalog, state and local open data portals

## How It Works

1. **Data Collection** - Government spending data is collected from public government records, official APIs, and budget reports
2. **Data Processing** - Data is normalized, categorized, and processed using AI technology powered by [Athena.live](https://athena.live)
3. **Publication** - All data is published as static HTML tables for public auditability and transparency
4. **No Interpretation** - Civic Datadog republishes verified public records without interpretation, estimates, or projections
