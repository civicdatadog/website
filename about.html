Mission

Civic Datadog exists to make public spending transparent, searchable, and accessible for everyone. We provide comprehensive access to government spending data, including federal spending, state spending, and local spending records compiled from verified public sources.

We are committed to neutrality, accuracy, and verifiable sourcing. Our platform focuses on government transparency, budget transparency, and fiscal transparency by republishing official government records without interpretation or bias. All data is sourced from public government APIs, budget reports, and financial disclosures.

Disclaimer

Civic Datadog republishes public data for transparency and accessibility. We aggregate government spending data from official sources including USAspending.gov, state budget offices, and local government financial records. All spending data is published as static HTML tables for public auditability and verification.

Civic Datadog does not provide financial advice, policy recommendations, or data interpretation. We serve as a transparency tool for government accountability and public access to spending information.
