Spending Categories

Explore comprehensive government spending data organized by jurisdiction. All spending tables are compiled from verified public government records, official budget reports, and government APIs for transparency and accountability.

  • Federal spending tables - Federal government spending data, agency obligations, and program budgets from USAspending.gov and federal budget sources
  • State spending tables - State government spending records, state agency budgets, and state-level fiscal data from official state sources
  • Local spending tables - City and county spending data, local government budgets, and municipal financial records from local government sources

All spending tables are static HTML compiled from public government records for public auditability and government transparency.

Notes

Government spending data is processed offline and published as static HTML tables for public auditability and transparency. All federal spending, state spending, and local spending records are sourced from official government APIs, budget reports, and public financial disclosures.

No dashboards, no real-time claims, no interpretation. Civic Datadog focuses on government transparency by providing verified, searchable spending data without bias or analysis.
